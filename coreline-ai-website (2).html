What Coreline AI Does

AI Staff Integration ▼

Automate tasks with AI employees and virtual assistants that work around the clock.

This is about replacing manual human labor at the task level, not the role level.

What we actually deploy

AI staff are configured to operate inside your business systems as if they were employees:

  • Phone system (inbound & outbound)
  • Website chat
  • SMS / WhatsApp
  • CRM
  • Calendar
  • Email
  • Internal dashboards

Each AI staff member has:

  • A defined role
  • A scope of responsibility
  • Decision rules
  • Escalation logic
  • Memory and context
  • Error-handling behavior

Example use cases

  • AI receptionist answers every call, qualifies the caller, books appointments, and flags emergencies.
  • AI intake agent handles new leads, asks qualifying questions, updates CRM, and schedules follow-ups.
  • AI support agent resolves common issues and routes complex cases to humans.

How this is different

Most "AI agents" stop at conversation. Ours take action. They don't just talk — they:

  • Write data
  • Trigger automations
  • Update records
  • Notify humans
  • Execute workflows

Result: Your business runs even when no one is online.

Workflow Automation ▼

Streamline repetitive processes and reduce errors with intelligent automation.

This is the backbone. Without this, AI is useless.

What we automate

We map your business into logic. Examples:

  • Lead submits form → AI qualifies → CRM updated → appointment booked → reminder sent
  • Customer calls → AI identifies intent → routes correctly → logs interaction
  • Missed call → AI calls back automatically → books appointment
  • Emergency detected → priority escalation → instant human transfer

Tools & logic

  • Conditional flows (if/else logic)
  • Time-based triggers
  • Data validation
  • Failover paths
  • Human-in-the-loop checkpoints

We design systems so nothing breaks silently.

Why this matters

Manual work causes:

  • Missed leads
  • Inconsistent follow-ups
  • Human error
  • Burnout

Automation removes all of that.

Result: Predictable operations, zero chaos, no micromanagement.
